About This Project

This Smart Healthcare System is designed to provide instant health analysis using a rule-based AI approach.

Users can input basic health parameters like blood pressure, oxygen level, sugar level, and pulse rate.

The system analyzes the data and provides meaningful health suggestions to help users take better decisions.

At [Healthcare+], we make healthcare simple and easy to understand.

Our platform helps you analyze medical reports and provides clear insights along with
personalized health suggestions.

We focus on helping you make better health decisions while keeping your data safe and secure.

Our mission is to empower you with the knowledge to take control of your health.s.
